Graphic Designer Career Path:
Creates visual content for print and digital media using design software.
Required Skills: Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign), Typography, Color theory, Layout design, Branding, User experience design
Education: Bachelor's in Graphic Design, Fine Arts, or related field
Experience Level: Junior Designer (0-2 years), Mid-level Designer (2-5 years), Senior Designer (5+ years)
Salary Range: ₹2-4 LPA (Junior), ₹4-8 LPA (Mid), ₹8-15 LPA (Senior)
Growth Path: Junior Designer → Senior Designer → Design Lead → Creative Director
