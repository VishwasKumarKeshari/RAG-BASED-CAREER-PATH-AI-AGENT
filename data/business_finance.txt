Financial Analyst Career Path:
Analyzes financial data to help businesses make investment decisions.
Required Skills: Financial modeling, Excel, Financial statements analysis, Valuation, Risk assessment, Bloomberg terminal
Education: Bachelor's in Finance, Accounting, Economics, or related field; CFA certification preferred
Experience Level: Entry-level (0-2 years), Mid-level (2-5 years), Senior (5+ years)
Salary Range: ₹4-8 LPA (Entry), ₹8-15 LPA (Mid), ₹15-30 LPA (Senior)
Growth Path: Senior Financial Analyst → Finance Manager → Director of Finance → CFO
