University Professor Career Path:
Teaches and conducts research at the university level in their field of expertise.
Required Skills: Subject expertise, Research methodology, Teaching, Academic writing, Grant writing, Student mentoring
Education: PhD in relevant field; Post-doctoral experience preferred
Experience Level: Assistant Professor (0-5 years), Associate Professor (5-10 years), Professor (10+ years)
Salary Range: ₹6-12 LPA (Assistant), ₹12-20 LPA (Associate), ₹20-40 LPA (Professor)
Growth Path: Assistant Professor → Associate Professor → Professor → Department Chair → Dean
