Doctor (General Physician) Career Path:
Diagnoses and treats general medical conditions and provides primary healthcare.
Required Skills: Medical diagnosis, Patient care, Pharmacology, Medical ethics, Communication, Emergency response
Education: MBBS degree; MD/MS specialization optional
Experience Level: Junior Doctor (0-2 years), Senior Resident (2-5 years), Consultant (5+ years)
Salary Range: ₹6-12 LPA (Junior), ₹12-25 LPA (Senior), ₹25-50 LPA (Consultant)
Growth Path: Junior Doctor → Senior Resident → Consultant → Senior Consultant → Chief of Department

---

Surgeon Career Path:
Performs surgical procedures to treat injuries, diseases, and deformities.
Required Skills: Surgical techniques, Anatomy, Physiology, Critical care, Precision, Decision making under pressure
Education: MBBS + MS/MD in Surgery; Super-specialization (MCh) optional
Experience Level: Junior Surgeon (0-2 years), Senior Resident (2-5 years), Consultant Surgeon (5+ years)
Salary Range: ₹8-15 LPA (Junior), ₹15-30 LPA (Senior), ₹30-60 LPA (Consultant)
Growth Path: Junior Surgeon → Senior Resident → Consultant Surgeon → Senior Consultant → Chief Surgeon

---

Nurse Career Path:
Provides patient care, administers medications, and assists physicians in medical procedures.
Required Skills: Patient care, Medication administration, Vital signs monitoring, Medical equipment operation, Communication, Emergency response
Education: Nursing diploma/BSc Nursing; MSc Nursing for specialization
Experience Level: Staff Nurse (0-2 years), Senior Nurse (2-5 years), Nurse Manager (5+ years)
Salary Range: ₹2-4 LPA (Staff), ₹4-8 LPA (Senior), ₹8-15 LPA (Manager)
Growth Path: Staff Nurse → Senior Nurse → Nurse Supervisor → Nurse Manager → Director of Nursing

---

Pharmacist Career Path:
Prepares and dispenses medications, provides drug information, and ensures safe medication use.
Required Skills: Pharmacology, Drug interactions, Patient counseling, Inventory management, Regulatory compliance, Compounding
Education: BPharm/MPharm degree
Experience Level: Junior Pharmacist (0-2 years), Senior Pharmacist (2-5 years), Chief Pharmacist (5+ years)
Salary Range: ₹2-4 LPA (Junior), ₹4-8 LPA (Senior), ₹8-15 LPA (Chief)
Growth Path: Junior Pharmacist → Senior Pharmacist → Pharmacy Manager → Chief Pharmacist

---

Radiologist Career Path:
Interprets medical images to diagnose and treat diseases using X-rays, CT, MRI, and ultrasound.
Required Skills: Image interpretation, Radiation safety, Anatomy, Pathology, Report writing, Technology proficiency
Education: MBBS + MD/DNB in Radiology
Experience Level: Junior Radiologist (0-2 years), Senior Resident (2-5 years), Consultant Radiologist (5+ years)
Salary Range: ₹8-15 LPA (Junior), ₹15-30 LPA (Senior), ₹30-50 LPA (Consultant)
Growth Path: Junior Radiologist → Senior Resident → Consultant Radiologist → Senior Consultant

---

Anesthesiologist Career Path:

Education: MBBS + MD/DNB in Anesthesiology
Experience Level: Junior Anesthesiologist (0-2 years), Senior Resident (2-5 years), Consultant (5+ years)
Salary Range: ₹8-15 LPA (Junior), ₹15-30 LPA (Senior), ₹30-50 LPA (Consultant)
Growth Path: Junior Anesthesiologist → Senior Resident → Consultant Anesthesiologist → Senior Consultant

Experience Level: Junior Lab Technician (0-2 years), Senior Technician (2-5 years), Lab Manager (5+ years)

Experience Level: Junior Therapist (0-2 years), Senior Therapist (2-5 years), Chief Physiotherapist (5+ years)

Psychiatrist Career Path:
Diagnoses and treats mental health disorders through medication and psychotherapy.

Experience Level: Junior Psychiatrist (0-2 years), Senior Resident (2-5 years), Consultant (5+ years)

Public Health Specialist Career Path:
Promotes and protects community health through education, policy, and disease prevention.
Required Skills: Epidemiology, Health policy, Community outreach, Data analysis, Program management, Research
Education: Master's in Public Health (MPH), MBBS + MPH, or related field
Experience Level: Program Officer (0-2 years), Senior Officer (2-5 years), Director (5+ years)
Salary Range: ₹4-8 LPA (Officer), ₹8-15 LPA (Senior), ₹15-30 LPA (Director)
Growth Path: Program Officer → Senior Program Officer → Program Director → Chief Public Health Officer
