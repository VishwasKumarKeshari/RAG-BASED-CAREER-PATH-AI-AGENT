Operations Manager Career Path:
Oversees daily operations of a business or department to ensure efficiency and productivity.
Required Skills: Process optimization, Team management, Budget management, Performance metrics, Problem solving, Strategic planning
Education: Bachelor's in Business, Operations Management, or related field; MBA preferred
Experience Level: Operations Coordinator (0-2 years), Operations Manager (2-5 years), Senior Operations Manager (5+ years)
Salary Range: ₹6-12 LPA (Coordinator), ₹12-20 LPA (Manager), ₹20-35 LPA (Senior)
Growth Path: Operations Coordinator → Operations Manager → Senior Operations Manager → Director of Operations
